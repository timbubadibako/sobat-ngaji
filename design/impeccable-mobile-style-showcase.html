Sobat Ngaji mobile design system showcase

Cool AI clarity, respectful Qur'an practice.

A mobile-first style direction for Sobat Ngaji before building the real Flutter screens: porcelain surfaces, ink-blue structure, aqua analysis states, and cool teal actions used consistently.

Physical scene

A learner practices after class in a quiet room at night. The app should feel focused, readable, and technically credible without turning recitation into a harsh test.

Design Tokens

BackgroundPure white so the UI does not drift into generic cream.
Ink StructureNavigation, hero surfaces, selected states, score text.
Aqua ActionPrimary actions and high-value progress moments.
AI SignalProcessing, waveform, analysis, and info states.
Quiet SurfaceToolbars, inactive tabs, selectors, and skeletons.
GeometryCards cap at 18px radius. Pills only for chips and badges.
TypeOne readable UI family. Arabic gets size, line-height, and contrast priority.
MotionOnly state feedback: recording pulse, processing, selected changes.
CopyUse evaluasi awal, kecocokan, perlu diulang, rekomendasi.

Screen Style Samples

These are not final app screens yet. They define the component vocabulary and visual behavior we can translate into Flutter.
